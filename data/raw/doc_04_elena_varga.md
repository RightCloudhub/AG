# Executive Biography: Elena Varga

Elena Varga is the CEO of Apex Holdings. Before Apex Holdings, Elena Varga worked at Orion Systems as VP of Strategy and later at Meridian Capital as Managing Director.

During her time at Orion Systems, Elena Varga collaborated with Marcus Chen on cloud product strategy. Marcus Chen later became CEO of NovaTech Industries.

Elena Varga founded no companies of her own but has served on the board of events related to Southeast Asia tech investment summits.
