# Acquisition Event: Harbor Components

In 2024, NovaTech Industries acquired a minority stake related partnership with Harbor Components, strengthening the QuantumEdge Server supply chain.

The event is recorded as the Harbor Partnership 2024. NovaTech Industries participated in Harbor Partnership 2024. Apex Holdings also participated in Harbor Partnership 2024 through its ownership of NovaTech Industries.

BrightLink Logistics was not an acquirer in this event but continued to supply logistics to NovaTech Industries.
