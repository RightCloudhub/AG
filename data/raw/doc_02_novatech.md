# NovaTech Industries

NovaTech Industries is a technology company and a subsidiary of Apex Holdings. NovaTech Industries is headquartered in Austin and focuses on enterprise computing.

The CEO of NovaTech Industries is Marcus Chen. Marcus Chen worked at Orion Systems from 2010 to 2016 and later founded a startup acquired by NovaTech Industries.

NovaTech Industries produces QuantumEdge Server and EdgeLite Workstation. QuantumEdge Server competes with products from Helix Compute.

Helix Compute is considered a competitor of NovaTech Industries in the enterprise server market.
